FAQs

What design services do you provide?
We design everything from logo to content managed websites. We also provide graphic service related to Packaging, Newspaper Ads, Billboards & Posters. Please contact us to discuss your project and design requirements.
How does the design process work, from my initial enquiry, to completion of the job?
We start all projects by thoroughly discussing the details of the project with the client. From this discussion we establish the specific requirements, which we call The Brief. We believe that client input is a crucial factor to making the project a success. Once we have established the brief, we begin work on the project by researching ideas. The design visuals are then forwarded onto the client for feedback. We listen carefully to client feedback, offering solutions and advice to any issues that may arise. If any alterations are required, these are made and the amended visual is resubmitted to the client. This process continues until we receive client approval.
What is the client’s involvement in the design process?
As an agency, we listen to our clients from the start. We encourage the client to bring in any samples, color combinations/swatches, and ideas they may have to help us get an idea of style before starting to work with new clients. During the design process, the client is asked to approve certain colors and fonts before work continues. Once the design project is ready for print, we will submit a final proof for the customer’s approval before submitting to production. Once a job is in production, the job cannot be cancelled. At the conclusion of a project, the client may request for the artwork in various formats if payment was made for design services.
How will I receive the finished design?
Once we have received the final payment, we will supply all electronic files on a Pendrive. For print jobs, you will also receive high resolution PDFs, which can be taken to a printer of your choice.
Will I be able to update these files on my computer?
Yes, you can with the help of the relevant software. The main software packages we use are Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop & Adobe InDesign.
Who owns the copyright for the designs you produce?
Copyright for all work carried out by us transfers to the client as soon as the balance has been settled in full. We do, however, reserve the right to use any work for promotional purposes.
Can you help us to develop our brand?
Yes, absolutely! Empowered Marketing is a full-service graphic design and digital marketing company with a wide range of professional services. We begin with a detailed questionnaire that helps us understand your company so we represent you clearly and effectively. Learn more about branding in a professional consultation.
